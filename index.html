How Might We?
As humans, solving problems, puzzles, and enigmas is one of our attributes. The Solvers. We need to solve our problems or somebody else, and learn from our choices, to move forward to the next phases. Understanding the problem is the first step. It leads us to the research stage of our journey. It gets "curiouser and curiouser", as Alice in Wonderland said. I am curious about how people work, interact and scale digital or IT products. I like both UX and UI work, as IÂ believe that they go hand in hand. Both have different skill sets needed by the parties. Both are powerful points of interaction that can get data-driven solutions through quantitative data and qualitative information. These results I turn these into prototypes and vote with my users on the most promising concept. Working in a team, as an Agile methodology, gives me much more understanding holistically of what each department is acting upon. This helps in keeping no room for inconsistencies or blockages. The end result is an ongoing updating our learnings, every time! It is a fun journey to be a member of this wonderful story.
