Scheduling System

Aeroflow scheduling system enables the Aero Dynamist principal to efficiently assign objectives, dates, and hours to multiple team members—while providing clear, real-time feedback and multi-channel notifications—then the team will experience improved clarity, reduced scheduling conflicts, and higher engagement with project objectives.

#fluidcognitive

#responsiveness

#designsystem

#usabilitytesting

#businessmetrics

#quanqualresearch

hypothesis

Aero Dynamist must have clear overview on scheduling objectives.

problem

Assigning complex, multi-week objectives to several aero dynamists, ensuring no conflicts, respecting hour limits, and providing clear visibility of assignments and missing hours.

empathy

As an aero dynamist or team member using the Aeroflow scheduling system, you want to feel confident that your objectives, assignments, and time commitments are clear and manageable. You value transparency—knowing exactly what is expected of you, how many hours you are assigned, and when your contributions are due.

define

Aero dynamists and their principal need a streamlined scheduling system that allows for the clear assignment of objectives, hours, and dates, with robust validation and notification features—without relying exclusively on email—to ensure all team members are informed, engaged, and able to collaborate on project goals.

solution

Calendar view for week/year navigation. Color coded assignments and hour summaries (e.g., 71/102 hours).Drag-and-drop or click-to-assign hours per aero dynamist.Real-time validation for hour limits and conflicts.“Add run” buttons for unassigned hours.

Touch-points

  • Entry point for scheduling and project overview
  • Assign objectives, dates, and hours with validation
  • Visualize assignments, missing hours, and navigate weeks/years
  • Real-time alerts for new or updated assignments
  • Manage notification preferences and view personal assignments

Outcome

The implementation of a user-centered Aeroflow scheduling system results in a highly transparent, efficient, and collaborative environment dashboard for aero dynamists and their teams. With clear assignment of objectives, real-time validation of hours, and multi-channel notifications, team members always know their responsibilities and time allocations without confusion or conflict.Aero dynamists experience increased confidence and autonomy, as they can instantly see their objectives, assigned hours, and deadlines in a visually intuitive calendar. Prompt, relevant notifications ensure that no assignment is missed, while color - coded summaries and real-time feedback prevent overbooking and highlight unassigned or missing hours.

IMF

Common Review System

The Fund aimed to modernise its outdated eReview system for managing reviews. Because the application was unhandy, the goal was to create a more intuitive and personalised experience to improve user comfort and trust.

#fluidcognitive

#responsiveness

#designsystem

#usabilitytesting

#businessmetrics

#quanqualresearch

hypothesis

People are overwhelmed by daily bureaucracy.

problem

Utilisation of outdated eReview system for managing reviews. Usability was unhandy, and the goal was to create a fluid and cognitive experience to improve user comfort and trust.

empathy

I conducted granular research to understand how users initiated and navigated the review process, identifying pain points with the legacy system. I discovered that users were frustrated by the lack of control, transparency, and clarity in managing and commenting on reviews.

define

Based on the findings, we defined the problem as a need for a more user-centric system. Users wanted greater control over the review process, with the ability to easily track the status of documents and make comments. They also needed features that were role-specific, tailored to the user's responsibilities within the organisation.

solution

Redesign the review management system, focusing on streamlining the document handling process and improving user engagement throughout the review stages.

Touch-points

  • New and modernised user experience
  • Improved departmental review management and online commenting
  • Includes all review stages from interdepartmental review to management clearance, corrections and deletions
  • Support for all Fund products, including FSAP, Safeguard Assessments and others
  • Enabled for all document security classifications, with permissions managed centrally within CRS

Usability Testing / Click testing

Example: 7 participants - played the role of the Author, Janice Mitchell. They were presented a Power Point Presentation in which we used the 10% (text) and 90% (image from our product) methodology. The users imagined that they were moving the cursor on the screen and pointing out by thinking out loud, were would they click, the reason why they did that action and what was their feeling completing a task. The session was moderated by facilitators, remote and lasted 45 minutes.

For this role, we tested how to Create Review, Prepare & Initiate Interdepartmental Review,​ Checking for comments from departments. After the test, the participants received a Word document for an over all usability of IMF product. This System Usability Scale (SUS) gave us a more comprehensive understanding of the user feedback by calculating a final score for each participant. This research report includes the details of how we conducted our research, the results we found, and a brief list of recommended changes.

Outcome

The redesigned system introduced a more structured review process, with stages clearly defined as Planned, Interdepartmental, and Clearance. This new system was a significant success, offering a more transparent, reliable, and user-friendly experience. The improved Common Review System helped the IMF build greater trust with users, leading to higher user satisfaction and confidence in the product.

Cambridge

Grading App

Develop a mobile application (compatible with both iOS and Android) that enables secure authentication for entering candidate results during exam sessions, with access based on their pre-scheduled booking dates.

#fluid

#mobiledesign

#designsystem

#mvpdesign

#businessmetrics

#quanqualresearch

hypothesis

People are not used anymore to grade students on paper based documents.  

problem

The paper based speaking (grading) mark sheet needs to be translated into a mobile application, with which an examiner can mark their speaking tests.

empathy

The examiner's role is to provide accurate and fair evaluations that reflect the individual's language abilities. They may also offer feedback and advice to help improve language skills. Overall, a language examiner plays a crucial role in assessing and promoting English language proficiency.

define

Once signed in, examiners can access a comprehensive dashboard that displays critical exam details, including:
> The application adopts a "first come first served" (FCFS) model for student seating. Examiners can easily visualise seating arrangements through an intuitive interface. This feature enhances transparency as it reflects real-time attendance and assists in maintaining order during the examination process.
> To simplify the grading task, the application provides a straightforward interface where examiners can view and select grading categories. Marks are allocated on a scale from 0 to 5.0, allowing examiners to assess students efficiently. The layout prioritises usability by making it easy to navigate through the grading categories, facilitating quick decision-making.
> After grading, the application empowers assessors by allowing them to send grades directly to students' email addresses with a single tap. This feature not only increases efficiency but also ensures that students receive timely feedback, fostering a transparent grading process.

solution

As a product designer, I am excited to present a preliminary concept for a mobile Minimum Viable Product (MVP) aimed at transforming the grading experience for examiners. This application prioritises clarity, transparency, usability, and fluidity in its interactions, ensuring a seamless operation from the moment examiners log in to the final submission of grades.

Touch-points

  • New and modernised user experience
  • Clear listing of all candidates being assessed.‍Location: Information about where each exam takes place.
  • Relevant details ensuring examiners are aware of the language needs of each student.
  • Scheduling details to aid in time management. Organising this information in a concise format allows examiners to quickly familiarise themselves with their tasks.

Outcome

This proposed mobile MVP embodies the principles of clarity, transparency, usability, and fluidity in its design. By focusing on the examiner's needs and streamlining the grading process, we can enhance user experience significantly while maintaining the integrity and security of the examination process.

Sobeys

Booking Appointment

Create a flow for Sobeys for their authenticated and guests' flows to book an appointment for their vaccination plan.

#fluid

#responsiveness

#mobiledesign

#designsystem

#usabilitytesting

#businessmetrics

#quanqualresearch

hypothesis

Providing a streamlined and intuitive appointment booking flow for Sobeys’ vaccination plan, enhances user satisfaction, reduce appointment scheduling errors, and increase the overall efficiency of the vaccination process.

problem

Current systems for booking vaccination appointments often present challenges in usability and accessibility. Many users are frustrated by complicated navigation, lengthy processes, and a lack of clear communication regarding appointment confirmations. This can lead to missed appointments, increased no-show rates, and lower overall participation in vaccination campaigns. Addressing these concerns is crucial for Sobeys to foster trust and engagement with the community.

empathy

This system is designed to accommodate both authenticated users and guests, ensuring a streamlined and efficient process for scheduling vaccinations. The flow prioritises clarity, ease of use, and timely confirmation of appointments.

define

The design challenge is to create a cohesive and user-friendly appointment booking flow that enhances the experience for both authenticated users and guests. Key user requirements include:
> Simple navigation and clear labeling throughout the booking process.
> Real-time availability of appointment slots based on location.
> A straightforward method for selecting vaccination services.
> Immediate and reliable confirmation of appointments via email.
> Reminders and follow-up communications to ensure users are informed and prepared for their appointments.

solution

A well-designed flow will cater to both authenticated users and guests, ensuring accessibility for all potential customers. By implementing this solution, Sobeys can improve the overall user experience, foster greater community engagement in vaccination efforts, and ensure a more organized and effective appointment booking process.

Touch-points

  • Users can access the appointment booking system via Sobeys' website or mobile application.
  • Select the service you want to book
  • Clear call-to-action buttons will guide users to start the appointment booking process, with distinct pathways for authenticated users and guests.
  • The system will highlight available locations based on the user's geographical area.

Outcome

This proposed flow for scheduling vaccination appointments at Sobeys emphasises a user-friendly experience for both authenticated and guest users. By incorporating intuitive design elements and clear communication throughout the process, I aim to ensure that users can effortlessly navigate the booking system, receive timely notifications, and engage with the vaccination plan confidently.

NTG Freight

Shipping services

Simplify shipping services from port to porch with NTG’s dedicated experts and industry-leading technology.

#fluid

#responsiveness

#mobiledesign

#designsystem

#usabilitytesting

#businessmetrics

#quanqualresearch

hypothesis

A carrier options manager for NTG will improve operational efficiency, reduce processing times, and enhance user satisfaction. By streamlining the user interface and providing clear visibility of carrier choices, users can make informed decisions rapidly, leading to a more effective freight management system.

problem

Current freight order management processes often involve cumbersome interfaces and lack the necessary tools to efficiently compare and select from various carrier options. Users are faced with difficulty in accessing comprehensive order details and evaluating carrier services, leading to increased time spent on decision-making, potential errors, and decreased productivity. This can ultimately impact service delivery and customer satisfaction.

empathy

Understanding the user experience is critical. Our research indicates that freight managers and logistics coordinators require quick access to detailed order information and clear visibility into carrier services to make timely decisions. These users often face pressure to optimise shipping costs and timelines while ensuring compliance with various regulations. By empathising with their challenges, we aim to develop a solution that not only meets functional needs but also supports their decision-making processes, minimizing stress during peak operational periods.

define

The core challenge is to create a comprehensive and user-friendly flow for managing freight orders and selecting carrier options. Essential user requirements include:
> Instant access to detailed freight order information, including shipment status, tracking details, and historical data.
> A streamlined interface for comparing and selecting carrier options based on criteria such as cost, transit time, and service reliability.
> The ability to update and modify orders efficiently without navigating away from the main interface.

solution

An integrated tool that allows users to filter and compare carrier options based on customisable criteria, including price, delivery speed, service ratings, and availability. By implementing these solutions, NTG can significantly enhance the operational efficiency of its freight order management system, improve user satisfaction, and create a more agile logistics environment.

Touch-points

  • Quick Filters: Ship Date Today, Ship date This Week.
  • Quote Number, Status, Customer Name, Origin ST, Destination ST, Shipping Service, Date Created, Lane Quote Price, Total Quote Price, Source, Created BY, Ship Date.

Outcome

A centralised dashboard where users can easily view all relevant order information in one place, complete with real-time status updates and historical shipment data.
